StoneGuard Privacy Policy

Your health data stays on your device. Always.

Effective date: May 11, 2026 Developer: Nicholas Haliday Android Health App

Overview

StoneGuard (“the App”) is a kidney stone prevention companion built by Nicholas Haliday (“Developer,” “we,” “us”). This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is stored, and your rights over it.

🔒 StoneGuard does not transmit your health data to any server. All personal health information is stored locally on your device and encrypted at rest.

Data You Enter (Stored Locally Only)

The following data is entered voluntarily by you and stored only on your device. It is never uploaded or shared:

  • Daily water intake logs
  • Dietary oxalate intake and food logs
  • Pain journal entries (pain level, side, notes, symptoms)
  • Stone-passed events
  • Historical progress data
  • App preferences (dark mode, daily hydration goal, notification settings)

How Your Data Is Protected

Your health data is protected using industry-standard encryption:

  • Journal entries are stored in an AES-256 encrypted SQLite database (SQLCipher). The encryption key is generated using a cryptographically secure random number generator and stored in Android’s secure hardware-backed Keystore via FlutterSecureStorage.
  • Daily history data is encrypted with AES-256-CBC using a fresh random IV on every save. The key is also stored in the secure Keystore.
  • Preferences (non-sensitive: theme, goal settings) are stored in SharedPreferences in plaintext.
Your encryption keys never leave your device and are not accessible to the Developer.

Third-Party Services

StoneGuard uses the following third-party SDKs. Each has its own privacy policy linked below:

  • Google AdMob — displays ads to support the free version of the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Google › Ads.
    Google Privacy Policy →
  • Google Fonts (Inter) — fonts are bundled with the app and loaded locally. No network requests are made for fonts at runtime.
  • Flutter Local Notifications — schedules and displays reminders entirely on-device. No data leaves your device.

App Permissions

StoneGuard requests only the permissions it needs:

  • Schedule Exact Alarms — required to deliver hydration reminders at the times you set. Used only for local notifications.
  • Post Notifications — required to show reminder notifications on Android 13+. No notifications are sent without your permission.
  • Internet — required solely for AdMob to load ads. Your health data is never transmitted over the internet.

Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

StoneGuard is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will provide guidance on deleting any locally stored data.

Deleting Your Data

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times:

  • You can delete individual journal entries from within the app.
  • You can reset all daily data from the Settings screen.
  • Uninstalling StoneGuard permanently removes all app data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes, we will notify you via an in-app message or Play Store update notes.
